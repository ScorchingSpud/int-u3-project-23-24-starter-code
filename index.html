Indecisive delineation

Eardrums assaulted by incessant screeching you begrudgingly sit up – vision bleary with eyelids drooping; exhaustion permating your very being you smack the top of your alarm clock – putting an end to its reign of terror. You stretch letting out a yawn as you do so. ‘6:00’ the foul beast’s screen reads. You let out a groan as for you your classes start at 9:00 but your mom signed you up for the school bus which dictates that you have to get ready by 6:30.

You fall back to sleep and by the time you wake up the wretched fiend's screen reads '7:20'. You pinch your nose, eyebrows furrowed in frustration -- what now?

Really wish I didn’t have to be a productive member of society… Despite everything you manage to get out of bed although grumpy and groggy it probably was for the best that you got up when you did…

You apologize to your bus matron and hustle through your routine. You can still make it but it’s frustrating nonetheless.

You go through your routine normally. Although still exhausted it feels good to get stuff done.
